Lucia — The Planner

Grit, hustle, and quiet precision. Lucia reads a room faster than most people read a headline—she’s the one who maps the exits and still walks out first.

Bio

Raised on the edge of Vice City’s sunburned suburbs, Lucia learned early that survival is a team sport. She keeps a tight circle, a tighter schedule, and a notebook full of ways out. When the heat rises, she gets calmer—counting seconds, signals, and camera arcs like a rhythm only she can hear.

  • Social engineering & disguise work
  • Dual-wield close-quarters control

Jason — The Wheelman

Fast talker. Faster driver. Jason turns chaos into clean lines—if there’s an opening, he’ll thread it at 120 and smile about it later.

Bio

Jason learned grip and throttle on cracked coastal roads and never shook the habit. He keeps the crew moving—vans, coupes, boats—whatever gets you from hot to gone. When the radios light up, he’s already picked three exits.

  • On-the-fly vehicle repairs
  • Close protection under fire
